Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app work offline?
Yes! PocketSous works completely offline. Once you've downloaded the app, you don't need an internet connection to access and use the recipes.
Q: How do I use the built-in timers?
When you reach a step that requires timing, you'll see a timer icon. Simply tap the timer button to start the countdown. The app will notify you when the time is up, even if your phone is locked.
Q: Will my screen turn off while I'm cooking?
No. PocketSous prevents your device from sleeping while you're actively using a recipe, so you don't have to worry about touching the screen with messy hands to keep it awake.
Q: Will more recipes be added?
Yes! We're continuously working on adding new recipes. Each recipe is carefully tested and formatted to provide the best cooking experience. Updates with new recipes will be released regularly.
Q: Can I suggest a recipe?
Absolutely! We love hearing what recipes you'd like to see in the app. Send your suggestions to support@pocketsous.app.
Q: What makes PocketSous different from other recipe apps?
PocketSous focuses on the cooking experience rather than just providing recipes. We break down each recipe into clear, manageable steps, include built-in timers, prevent your screen from sleeping, and design the interface for one-handed use while cooking. Q: Can I customize the recipes?
Currently, PocketSous provides fixed recipes, but we're exploring ways to add customization options in future updates.
